MAIANA®-Wifi

WebFlasher

Flash or Find your device using next options:
  1. Plug in your ESP32 to a USB port.
  2. Hit "Install Full" for inital load (includes JFIFF files) or "Firmware only for firmware only update" and select the correct COM port.
  3. Get firmware installed and connected in less than 3 minutes!
  4. Connect to the MAIANA wifi and configure the wifi network to connect your device to.
Note. The connect & log option in the webflasher is not supported by the MAIANA firmware. (due to the difference in preset baudrate)
